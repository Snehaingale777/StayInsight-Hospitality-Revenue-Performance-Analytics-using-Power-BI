# StayInsight-Hospitality-Revenue-Performance-Analytics-using-Power-BI
📊 StayInsight – Hospitality Revenue & Performance Analytics (Power BI)
📌 Project Overview

StayInsight is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze hospitality industry data and monitor hotel business performance.
The dashboard provides insights into key metrics such as Revenue, RevPAR, ADR, Occupancy Rate, and Realisation %, helping stakeholders understand trends and optimize decision-making.

🎯 Project Objectives

Analyze hotel revenue and operational performance.

Identify trends in occupancy and booking behavior.

Compare weekday vs weekend performance.

Evaluate revenue distribution across cities, properties, and booking platforms.

Provide actionable business insights for revenue optimization.

📈 Key Metrics Used

Revenue – Total realized revenue from hotel bookings

RevPAR (Revenue per Available Room) – Measures hotel revenue efficiency

ADR (Average Daily Rate) – Average revenue earned per booked room

Occupancy Rate – Percentage of rooms occupied

Realisation % – Percentage of successful bookings

📊 Dashboard Features

✔ Interactive KPI cards for quick performance monitoring
✔ Revenue and occupancy analysis by city and property
✔ Trend analysis by week and month
✔ Booking platform performance comparison
✔ Category-wise occupancy visualization
✔ Dynamic filters for city, room class, and time period

1️⃣ What problem does this dashboard solve?

This dashboard helps hotel management monitor key business metrics like Revenue, RevPAR, ADR, Occupancy Rate, and Realisation %.
It enables stakeholders to analyze hotel performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions to improve profitability.

2️⃣ What is RevPAR and why is it important?

RevPAR (Revenue per Available Room) measures how efficiently a hotel generates revenue from its available rooms.

Formula:

RevPAR = Revenue / Total Room Capacity

It combines both occupancy and pricing performance into a single metric.

3️⃣ What is ADR?

ADR (Average Daily Rate) represents the average revenue earned per booked room.

Formula:

ADR = Revenue / Total Bookings

It helps measure the pricing strategy effectiveness.

4️⃣ What is Occupancy Rate?

Occupancy Rate measures the percentage of available rooms that are occupied.

Formula:

Occupancy % = Successful Bookings / Total Capacity

This metric helps evaluate hotel demand and utilization.

5️⃣ What is Realisation %?

Realisation % measures the proportion of successful bookings compared to total bookings.

Formula:

Realisation % = Successful Bookings / Total Bookings

It indicates how many bookings were successfully completed without cancellations.
